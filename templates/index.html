Welcome to IBRI's Toxicogenomic Platform

Project Goals: Generate a shared platform for early risk assessment using toxicogenomics that can accelerate the development of the supporting science and increase adoption of new risks assessment tools by the broader research community. This phase of the research project will instantiate the secure collaboration tools across the participating organizations, define the conceptual architecture and framework that leverages and/or integrates with other efforts in toxicogenomics, and generates the first working implementation of this platform to deliver a full end-to-end workflow from data to visualizations with to-be-defined research methods based on the best practices of the Sponsor(s).
