CTox, a shared toxicogenomic platform hosted by the Indiana Biosciences Research Institute, can help answer all those questions and more. It delivers a full end-to-end workflow – from data to visualizations – for early risk assessment using toxicogenomics. The data sets that exist within CTox are based on publicly available data sources.
